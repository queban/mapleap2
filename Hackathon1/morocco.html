Customs:

Moroccans eat family-style meals while sitting on the floor around a large dish of couscous, meat, and vegetables. They use their right hands to take food from the dish, and soak up leftover juices with bread. Soups are eaten with spoons.

Trivia:

In Morocco, the liver is considered the symbol of love, rather than the heart.
