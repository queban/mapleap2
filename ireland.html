Customs:

The Puck Fair: A celebration where a wild goat is crowned “King Puck” and celebrated for three days.

Trivia:

Saint Patrick was not Irish: The patron saint of Ireland was born in Britain to a Romanized family and didn't set foot in Ireland until he was 16.
